# Questions for Tom and Ben

## Start with Tom

1. What part of MoviesMadeEasy matters most to you: theaters, streaming, classics, trailers, simplicity, or nostalgia for moviegoing?
2. When you picture the first user, who are they?
3. Are they older movie fans, families, casual streamers, theatergoers, classic-film people, or everyone?
4. What should the app never become?
5. Which feature would disappoint you most if it were not in version 1?
6. Which feature could wait until version 2?

## Ask Ben for useful skepticism

1. What is the biggest reason this would not work?
2. What is the clearest customer segment?
3. What would make this marketable in one sentence?
4. What proof would you need before spending real money?
5. Would you rather validate with user tests, a landing page, or a small clickable/mobile prototype?
6. What budget ceiling feels comfortable for the next phase?

## Data-source questions for any developer/vendor

1. What exact source powers movie metadata?
2. What exact source powers trailers?
3. What exact source powers streaming availability?
4. What exact source powers theater showtimes?
5. Are API/data costs included or passed through separately?
6. Are links affiliate links, deep links, search links, or partner integrations?
7. What happens if a provider changes pricing or terms?
8. Can providers be swapped without rebuilding the app?
9. What usage analytics will prove retention and outbound intent?
10. What is explicitly out of scope for version 1?

## Decision question for the end

```text
Do we agree that the next phase is a short validation sprint around the MVP, rather than a full production app build?
```
