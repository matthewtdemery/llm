Competitive baseline test

Find a movie, preview it, then choose the right place to watch.

  1. Search or pick a list.
  2. Watch the trailer first.
  3. Choose theater, streaming, rent, or buy.

Inspired by Tom’s Apple App Store reference, but intentionally clearer about what the app does: it helps you decide, it does not pretend every movie is free or instantly playable.
