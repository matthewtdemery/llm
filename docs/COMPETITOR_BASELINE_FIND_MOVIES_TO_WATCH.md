# Competitor / Baseline Reference: Find movies to watch

Checked UTC: 2026-07-01T17:49:15.582420+00:00

Source: Apple App Store listing for `Find movies to watch`.

## Why this matters

Tom flagged this app as close to the product direction and suggested it may be useful as a baseline template. It is useful as a **competitive baseline**, not something to copy directly.

## What the listing says

- App name: Find movies to watch
- Category: Entertainment
- Price: Free with in-app purchases/subscription options
- Developer: Coodly LLC / Coodly OU
- App Store rating shown during check: 3.5 out of 5 across 567 ratings
- Core promise: help users browse movies and see where they are available to stream, rent, or buy
- Data note: listing says it uses TMDb API and is not endorsed/certified by TMDb
- Supported surfaces shown: iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV

## Review/comment themes seen on App Store page

Positive signal:

- Users want a simple place to search for movies and decide what to watch.

Negative signals / opportunity:

- Several comments show confusion about whether the app lets users actually watch movies.
- Users complain when search/discovery does not lead to a clear viewing action.
- Some reviews mention weak or misleading movie selection.
- One review explicitly complains about lack of trailers / being sent to iTunes/payment expectations.

## Implications for MoviesMadeEasy / FindAMovie

1. Do not position the app as “watch free movies.”
2. Make the promise extremely clear: discover, watch trailer, then choose theater/streaming route.
3. Keep trailers central, because review pain points suggest users expect previews before committing.
4. Make availability/routing honest: “where to watch” must distinguish trailer, theater, streaming, rent/buy, and unavailable.
5. Do not clone the layout; use it as a baseline for what users already recognize and where they get frustrated.
6. Tom’s differentiator should remain: movies-only, no TV clutter, profile-driven filtering, trailer-first decision path, and clear route to watch.
