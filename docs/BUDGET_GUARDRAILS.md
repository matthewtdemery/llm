# Budget Guardrails

## The main distinction

```text
A validation demo is cheap. A production app with reliable data, routing, support, maintenance, and marketing is not cheap.
```

## Use these buckets in conversation

### Validation prototype / MVP lab

Purpose:

- prove user reaction;
- sharpen the product story;
- reduce risk before vendor spend.

Current status:

- already exists locally.

### Production MVP

Purpose:

- real users;
- real metadata/trailer data;
- limited streaming/theater routing;
- accounts/watchlist;
- analytics.

Guardrail:

```text
Do not price this until provider assumptions are explicit.
```

### Full app

Purpose:

- broader coverage;
- native apps;
- stronger marketing;
- partnerships;
- support operations.

Guardrail:

```text
Do not jump here from the idea stage.
```

## Budget language for the call

Use:

```text
I would separate the next phase from the full build. The next phase should be evidence-gathering. Once we know users care, then a vendor quote becomes much more meaningful.
```

Avoid:

```text
This will definitely cost X and make Y.
```

Better:

```text
Any quote that does not specify metadata, streaming availability, showtimes, ticketing/deeplinks, hosting, maintenance, and marketing assumptions is incomplete.
```
